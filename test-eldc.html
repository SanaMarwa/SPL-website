ELDC

Welcome to our exciting "Education & Life skills Development Course" designed to foster holistic development in children.

At S.P.L. School, our educational program is the heart of our mission to nurture well-rounded individuals. Through a structured curriculum, we ignite a love for learning while developing crucial skills in reading, writing, speaking, and critical thinking.

Our program embraces holistic growth, empowering students to excel academically and thrive in all areas of life. As they conquer each level, students unlock new challenges and opportunities, fostering a sense of accomplishment that paves the way for their continued success.

LEVEL - 1

Reading Capability

Building Strong Foundations:

Your child will develop the essential ability to read fluently, opening doors to endless knowledge and imagination.

LEVEL - 2

Writing Proficiency

Expressing Ideas with Precision:

With a solid foundation in comprehension, your child will progress to write confidently and creatively, conveying their thoughts without relying on memorization.

LEVEL - 3

Speaking Skills

Captivating Audiences:

Building upon their reading skills, your child will enhance their comprehension and speaking abilities, enabling them to effectively express themselves and engage in meaningful conversations.

LEVEL - 4

Visualizing Concepts

Unleashing Creativity:

At this level, your child will learn to convert their understanding into visual representations. They will draw connections and relationships between keywords, creating diagrams that vividly showcase their comprehension.

LEVEL - 5

Critical Thinking

Unleashing Intellectual Power:

Encouraging critical thinking, your child will analyze factual content in subjects like science, as well as learn to validate new information in areas such as social studies and history.

LEVEL - 6

Creative Problem-Solving

Innovating for Real-World Solutions:

Equipped with new knowledge, your child will develop problem-solving skills. They will utilize their understanding to solve real-world problems and apply their knowledge to make a positive impact.

LEVEL - 7

Collaboration

Thriving Together:

In the final level, your child will focus on developing emotional intelligence and effective communication skills. They will learn to listen to others' perspectives, identify logical flaws, and effectively communicate their ideas while considering compromises to achieve common goals.

Our program, conducted four times a week, offers every student a unique opportunity to participate and grow. Alongside regular classes, we allocate dedicated periods for practicing the program in languages besides English, like Hindi, Telugu, and Urdu. The program is implemented across all languages, incorporating content from English, Conversation books, Science, and Social studies.

We believe in promoting progress and ensuring that when a child consistently excels at a level, they are ready to embark on the next thrilling stage of their educational journey.
